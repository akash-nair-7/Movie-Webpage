MINNAL

MURALI

"Minnal Murali" is an Indian superhero film directed by Basil Joseph and starring Tovino Thomas. The story revolves around Murali, a young man who gains extraordinary powers after being struck by lightning. Adopting the persona of Minnal Murali, he fights against injustice while juggling the challenges of his personal life. With impressive visuals, thrilling action sequences, and a vibrant portrayal of Kerala's culture, the film offers a refreshing take on the superhero genre in Indian cinema. It explores themes of heroism, identity, and the power of individuals to create change. Featuring a talented ensemble cast, including Guru Somasundaram and Aju Varghese, "Minnal Murali" is a must-watch for fans of superheroes and Indian cinema, delivering an exciting and entertaining cinematic experience.
